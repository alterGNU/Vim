- Will trying to solve [Install. taskwiki pb](../Notes/Tools/Vim/Taskwiki/How_to_install_vimplugin_taskwiki.md) I learned:

- In Vim, to Search&Replace a char **[X]** by **\n** :
    - `%s:/[X]/^M/g` : To insert ^M `[Ctrl]+[v]+[Enter]`
